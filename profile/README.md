[Xemantic](https://xemantic.com) is our applied philosophy collective. We are:

- independent AI research lab
- mathematics of perception research lab
- computational art studio

Xemantic was founded by [Julia Thomas](https://github.com/uncannyJulia) and [Kazik Pogoda](https://github.com/morisil) and is focused on writing humanistic code for the betterment of the society.

We also co-founded [Prachtsaal](https://prachtsaal.berlin), a non-profit cultural center in Berlin where we reside. See also [Prachtsaal on GitHub](https://github.com/prachtsaal/).

## What We Build

- [Golem XIV](https://github.com/xemantic/golem-xiv) - Autonomous metacognitive AI system with semantic memory and self-directed research (AGPL, coming soon)
- [Claudine](https://github.com/xemantic/claudine) - Autonomous AI agent for your command line (winner of AI hack Berlin hackathon at Google)
- [xemantic-ai](https://github.com/xemantic/xemantic-ai) - Open source AI libraries for Kotlin
- [OPENRNDR](https://openrndr.org/) - Contributing to the creative coding framework for Kotlin ([GitHub](https://github.com/openrndr/openrndr))
- [404](https://xemantic.com/404/) - Immersive performance exploring feedback loops between human and artificial intelligence
